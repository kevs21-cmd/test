Admission Procedures

Freshman Applicants

  • 1

    Complete Online Application

    Fill out the application form on the PGPC portal with your personal and academic information.

  • 2

    Submit Required Documents

    Upload high school transcripts, recommendation letters, and other required documents.

  • 3

    Take Entrance Examination

    Register and take the DLSU College Admission Test (DCAT) on your scheduled date.

  • 4

    Await Results and Confirm Enrollment

    Check admission results online and confirm your enrollment if accepted.

Transfer Applicants

  • 1

    Complete Transfer Application

    Fill out the transfer application form on the DLSU portal with your academic history.

  • 2

    Submit College Transcripts

    Provide official transcripts from your previous college or university.

  • 3

    Course Evaluation

    Your previous coursework will be evaluated for potential credit transfer.

  • 4

    Interview and Enrollment

    Complete an interview with the department and confirm enrollment if accepted.

Student Experience

Life at PGPC extends far beyond the classroom. Our vibrant campus community offers countless opportunities for personal growth, leadership development, and meaningful connections. With over 80 student organizations, sports teams, and cultural groups, there's something for everyone.

Our state-of-the-art facilities include modern libraries, research centers, sports complexes, and creative spaces that support both academic excellence and student life. Student Support

At PGPC, we are committed to supporting our students throughout their academic journey. Our comprehensive support services include academic advising, career counseling, health and wellness programs, and financial aid options to ensure that every student has the resources they need to succeed.

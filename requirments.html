Enrollment Requirements

Academic Year 2025-2026

General Requirements

  • Duly accomplished Application Form
  • Original Report Card (Form 138) / Transcript of Records with good academic standing
  • Original PSA Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character (issued within the last 6 months)
  • 4 pieces of recent 2x2 ID pictures with white background
  • Medical Certificate with complete physical examination results

Additional Requirements

For Transfer Students:

  • Official Transcript of Records from previous school
  • Certificate of Transfer Credential / Honorable Dismissal
  • Course description of all subjects taken

For International Students:

  • Alien Certificate of Registration (ACR)
  • Student Visa or appropriate visa
  • Passport copy with valid visa

Important Notes

  • All documents must be original or certified true copies
  • Documents in foreign languages must be accompanied by an English translation from an accredited translator
  • Incomplete requirements will not be processed
  • Submission deadline: July 31, 2025

About Padre Garcia Polytechnic College

Our Heritage

Founded in 2024, Padre Garcia Polytechnic College is a premier college established by the LGU. we have been committed to fostering academic excellence, innovative research, and service to society.

Academic Excellence

PGPC offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across various disciplines. Our university consistently ranks among the top educational institutions in the Philippines and Asia, with numerous programs receiving Level IV accreditation.

Campus Life

Experience a vibrant campus life with over 50 student organizations, state-of-the-art facilities, and a supportive community. Our campus in the Bry. Castillio provides an ideal environment for holistic development and growth.

Quick Enrollment Guide

Enrollment Steps:

  1. Complete the online application form
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Take the entrance examination
  4. Attend the interview (if required)
  5. Wait for admission results
  6. Confirm enrollment and pay fees

Basic Requirements:

  • High school transcript of records
  • Certificate of good moral character
  • Birth certificate
  • 2x2 ID photos (4 pieces)
  • Application fee payment receipt
